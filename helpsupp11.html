Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How does the voting process work?

Once logged in, navigate to the 'Election' page to view candidates and vote for each position.

2. Is my vote secure?

Yes, the KSK Voting System uses secure protocols to ensure your vote remains confidential and tamper-proof.

3. How do I manage my account details?

You can manage your account details under the 'Settings' section after logging in.
